About Us

State Road Salvage was established in the early 50’s. After changing hands a few times over the years the company resides with its current owner Brian Moody. Who reopen State Road Salvage in July of 2014. Since the reopening of State Road Salvage there has been many changes. We offer a more friendly environment to our customers and try to provide the best service possible. The team that works here is very knowledgeable and offers professional assistance to help each of our customers. The owner Brian Moody is constantly thinking of new and improved ways to make this business more appealing to our customers. He has been an entrepreneur for almost 20 years now and has gained incredible insight in terms of business. First of all, knowing that nothing is more important to him when it comes to business than the customers. Because without the individuals that we provide services to we would not be able to make a dime. As a growing business we want all our customers to feels as tho they are treated with the utmost respect. We know that the future of our company relies on the wellness of our customers to come back and spread the word about our company.
